Below are four representative projects from my translational neurotechnology portfolio. Scroll horizontally to explore each one.

Next Generation Non-invasive Brain stimulation

Ultra–high-density pulsed electrical stimulation arrays capable of sculpting superficial electric fields with deep penetration. Validated in rodents, cadavers, and early human studies.

Non-invasiveHigh-density arraysField steering

FloaTES: Minimally Invasive Deep Brain Neuromodulation

Floating microwire bundles designed to couple surface currents into deep nuclei (STN, NAc, VTA) while minimizing mechanical mismatch, inflammation, and surgical burden.

Minimally invasiveDeep targetsNetwork DBS

Sensory-based deep brain stimulation

Goal is to achieve targeted neuromodulation without invasive electrodes.Sensory stimulation uses peripheral sensory pathways to indirectly modulate deep brain circuits.Modalities: visual, auditory, somatosensory, olfactory, or multisensory. .

Retinal Stimulatio using Ultrasonically enhanced OCT

Using ultrasound-induced refractive index gradients to steer light inside the eye, enabling structured retinal stimulation and simultaneous OCT imaging.
